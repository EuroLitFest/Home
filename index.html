A fest like never before!

This year's litfest promises to be one like never before. There are several new events which have been introduced, of which some are listed:

Tech-O-Tank

In a world ridden by the crippling horrors of chaos, devoured by mayhem and threatened by the forces of malice, it takes a real hero to save the day. Racing against time in the quest to find the answer, technology is your only weapon. Tech-o-Tank is the definition of kindling the mind. Players use luck, precision, innovation and simply every inch of their capacity to create solutions for problems that plague society.

Battle of Bands

Music is the essence of life. Everything that has rhythm has music. Battle of the Bands is a music competition in which two or more bands compete for the title of "best band". Irrespective of the language and genre, various artists come together to take part and create an environment to exhibit their dexterous talent and showcase the emergence of amazing talents and diverse musical acts.

Wolf of Skool Street

Inviting the dynamic entrepreneurs of tomorrow to showcase their innovative skills and be enthralled in the experience of charting out a plan in creating their first wealth Come bask in the glory of being in the shoes of a CEO of your own home-made business venture or be the ‘Senapati’ of your own business.
